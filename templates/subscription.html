Features

Dashboard A summary of the trader's sales, inventory, expenses, and other key metrics.
Sales management A table to add, edit, and delete sales transactions, with columns for the date, customer name, product name, quantity, and price.
Inventory Management A table to add, edit, and delete products, with columns for the product name, description, quantity, and price
Expense Management A table to add, edit, and delete expenses, with columns for the date, category, and amount.
Ledger Management A table to manage the trader's financial transactions, including income and expenses, with columns for the date, description, and amount.
Reports Reports based on the trader's sales, inventory, expenses, and other data. This includes profit and loss statements, sales reports, and inventory reports.
